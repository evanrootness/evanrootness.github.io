Where I'm from

I come from Rockford, MN, a small town located in some corn fields about an hour West of the Twin Cities.

What I'm up to

I'm currently a senior at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor, where I study Astronomy and Physics. When I'm not studying for some physics midterm I'm probably researching the coolest astronomical object out there, globular star clusters. My current research focuses on discovering new globular clusters in the Andromeda Galaxy. I'm also in the Michigan Data Science Team; a fun, project-based data science club at UMich.

What I like to do

In my free time I like to work on data science projects to get more coding practice and do cool projects like my Etsy art listing project. Recently, I've been doing a lot more painting in my free time. I've been focusing more on the oil medium recently, which you should go check out in The Gallery. Other things I enjoy doing in my free time include: reading, playing different instruments (piano, trumpet, guitar), playing sports outside (volleyball, frisbee, spikeball), and enjoying nature.
